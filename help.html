Help Center

Need a hand? Here’s everything you need to know to get the most out of your Princeton Laundry experience.

Using Our Machines

  • Insert your card or quarters into the payment system on the machine.
  • Select your cycle based on the type of clothes (Normal, Delicates, Heavy, etc.).
  • Add detergent, load your laundry, and press Start.
  • Wash cycle usually takes ~30 minutes; Drying takes 30–45 minutes depending on load.

Troubleshooting Common Problems

  • Machine didn’t start? Ensure you pressed the final Start button after payment.
  • Clothes still damp? You may need an extra dry cycle for heavier loads like towels or blankets.

Something Went Wrong?

  • Request a refund: Let staff know or contact us with machine number, issue, and approximate time.
  • Left your clothes? Items are kept in lost & found for 7 days.
  • Found someone else's laundry? Please turn it in to the staff counter.

Laundry Tips for Better Results

  • Don’t overload machines – it reduces cleaning effectiveness.
  • Use high-efficiency (HE) detergent sparingly – more soap ≠ cleaner clothes.
  • Separate whites and colors to avoid dye transfer.
  • Use mesh bags for delicates or baby clothes.
